Agriculture Data

Agriculture plays a vital role in the Indian economy, with nearly 60% of the population engaged in farming and allied activities. Here are some key statistics:

  • 145 million farmers contribute to agriculture.
  • Top crops: Rice, Wheat, Sugarcane, Cotton, Pulses.
  • Agriculture contributes 18% to the national GDP.
  • India is a top producer of milk, spices, and horticultural crops.
